Applied to Hearts Academy? Wondering what happens next?

We believe in transparency, especially when you're making a big career move. Our selection process isn't about arbitrary grades; it's about finding the next generation of engineers ready to build and lead.

Here's exactly what to expect:

1.  **Application Review:** We carefully evaluate your submission. We look for drive, potential, and a hunger to build. Your passion for creating real software speaks louder than any transcript.
2.  **Technical Challenge:** If your application stands out, we'll send a practical, take-home challenge. This isn't about memorizing algorithms – it's about problem-solving and how you approach real-world coding scenarios.
3.  **Interview:** Clear the challenge? We'll invite you for a conversation. This is where we get to know your ambitions and ensure a fit for our intensive, 8-10 person cohort. We're senior engineers, and we're looking for individuals we can truly mentor to industry readiness.
4.  **Offer & Feedback:** Successful candidates receive an offer to join. If it's not the right fit this time, we provide constructive feedback to help you on your journey.

We set a high bar because we're preparing you for impact, not just another certificate.

Applications close April 20. Don't wait. Your portfolio starts here.
