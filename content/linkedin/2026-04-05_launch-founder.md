### Why We Built Hearts Academy: Closing the Industry Gap for Sri Lanka's Engineers

**By the Founders of Hearts Academy**

For years, we've hired, mentored, and built engineering teams across Sri Lanka's tech landscape. We've seen incredible talent. But we've also seen a recurring, frustrating gap: talented graduates struggling to transition from academic knowledge to industry-ready performance. They had degrees, but lacked deployed software. They knew theory, but hadn't shipped code that mattered.

This wasn't a problem with effort or intelligence. It was a problem with the pipeline. Traditional education, while foundational, often falls short in preparing engineers for the pace, demands, and specific tech stacks of a modern team. Bootcamps promise a lot, but often deliver a superficial understanding that still leaves graduates unprepared for the real world.

We remember that feeling of being a student, eager to contribute, but without the practical experience or mentorship to truly break in. We saw the need for a different path. A path built by senior engineers, for the next generation of engineers. That's why we founded Hearts Academy.

**Our Core Belief: Your Portfolio > Your Transcript**

We believe what truly matters to an employer isn't your GPA, but the code you've written, the problems you've solved, and the projects you've shipped. At Hearts Academy, you won't just study. You'll build. Over 24 intensive weeks, you'll work on real-world projects, using industry-standard tech stacks, culminating in a robust portfolio of deployed software. This isn't theoretical; it's tangible.

**Real Mentorship, Not Just Instruction**

You won't learn from TAs or recent graduates. You'll be directly mentored by senior engineers who have built and led teams in production environments. We guide you, challenge you, and push you to develop the problem-solving skills and code quality demanded by top companies. Our small cohort size of 8-10 people ensures no one gets lost, and everyone receives personalized, hands-on attention.

**Preparing for the Agentic Era**

The rise of AI isn't just a trend; it's a paradigm shift. We’re not just teaching you to code; we’re preparing you to lead AI-powered teams. Hearts Academy focuses on developing engineers who understand how to leverage AI tools, integrate them into workflows, and architect solutions in this new, agentic era. You will be ready for the future, not just the present.

**Performance Rewards: We Invest in Your Success**

We set a high bar because we know what you're capable of. We also believe in rewarding excellence. Top performers in our program pay less—or nothing at all—for their education. This isn't a gimmick; it's our commitment to investing in the best talent and ensuring access to those who truly earn it.

We built Hearts Academy because we refuse to see another talented Sri Lankan engineer struggle to find their place. We built it to create a direct, proven pathway from student to industry-ready engineer. We built it to challenge the status quo and offer a better way forward.
