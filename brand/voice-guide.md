# Hearts Academy — Brand Voice Guide

## Brand Personality

**Archetype:** The Mentor (primary) + The Challenger (secondary)

We're not a traditional bootcamp. We're senior engineers who care about the next generation. We speak with authority but without arrogance. We challenge the status quo of CS education while offering a clear, proven path forward.

## Voice Attributes

1. **Direct** — No fluff. Say what you mean.
2. **Confident** — We know this works because we've seen it work.
3. **Empathetic** — We remember what it felt like to be a student with zero industry experience.
4. **Ambitious** — We set a high bar and help you clear it.
5. **Real** — No corporate speak. No empty promises.

## Tone Guidelines

### DO
- Use "you" and "we" — make it personal
- Lead with the problem students actually face
- Back claims with specifics (24 weeks, 8-10 cohort, real tech stack)
- Use active voice
- Short sentences. Punchy paragraphs.
- Include social proof (testimonials, outcomes)

### DON'T
- Say "world-class" or "cutting-edge" — everyone says that
- Use excessive exclamation marks
- Promise guaranteed jobs (say "placement support" / "get you hired")
- Be condescending about university education
- Use jargon without context

## Key Messages (Priority Order)

1. **Portfolio > Transcript** — You graduate with deployed software, not just a GPA
2. **AI is here** — The agentic era needs engineers who can lead AI-powered teams
3. **Real mentorship** — Senior engineers, not TAs
4. **Performance rewards** — Top performers pay less (or nothing)
5. **Small cohort** — 8-10 people. No one gets lost.
